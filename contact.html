FAQ

Common questions

Is NeuralDecide really free?
The core simulator is completely free and requires no signup. You can run unlimited simulations in your browser. We offer a Pro plan for teams, advanced analytics, and decision history sync across devices.
How is the success probability calculated?
We use a weighted scoring model: (Σ Pro_weight × Pro_certainty − Σ Con_weight × Con_certainty) / TotalPossibleScore × 100. The formula is designed to be transparent — you can see every input that drives the output. No magic, just math.
Do you offer enterprise plans?
Yes. Enterprise plans include collaborative decision rooms (multiple stakeholders), custom factor templates, outcome tracking, audit logs, and dedicated support. Email enterprise@neuraldecide.com to get started.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Simulations run locally in your browser by default. Nothing is sent to our servers unless you're signed in and explicitly save to cloud. We will never sell or share your decision data with anyone.
Can I integrate this with my own tools?
Our API is in private beta. Enterprise customers can integrate NeuralDecide into their existing workflows via REST API. Apply by emailing enterprise@neuraldecide.com.
