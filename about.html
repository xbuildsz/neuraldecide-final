OUR JOURNEY

How we got here

2021

The Idea

After a costly product launch failure, our founders sketched the first weighted decision model on a whiteboard. The question: can you quantify whether a decision is actually good?

2022

First Version

A basic spreadsheet tool shared in a startup Slack. Within a week, 300 founders had copied it. We realized we were onto something real.

2023

Seed Round & Launch

Raised $2.4M. Rebuilt the engine from scratch with real-time processing, risk heatmaps, and the inversion thinking module. Launched publicly.

2024

10,000 Users

Hit five figures. Enterprise customers started adopting NeuralDecide for board-level strategic decisions. Dashboard and team features shipped.

Now

The Future

Building collaborative decision rooms, AI-assisted factor generation, and outcome tracking. The best is ahead of us.

OUR VALUES
