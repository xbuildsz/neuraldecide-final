WEIGHTED SCORING ENGINE

Math beats intuition

Every factor you add — pro or con — gets a weight (1–10, how important is this?) and a certainty score (1–10, how sure are you?). The engine multiplies them, aggregates the results, and spits out a precision score.

The formula is: Score = (ΣPro_w×c − ΣCon_w×c) / MaxPossible × 100

INVERSION THINKING

Think backwards to move forward

Inspired by Charlie Munger's mental model: instead of only asking "how do I succeed?", we also ask "how would I most likely fail?" The inversion prompt is built into every report — and it catches the blind spots your optimism misses.
